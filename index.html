About Company

Advanced Boiler & PRDS Solutions for Energy-Efficient Industrial Systems

we specialize in delivering high-performance Boiler and Pressure Reducing & Desuperheating Systems (PRDS) tailored for power, sugar, distilleries, textile, paper, oil & gas, and other industrial sectors. Our PRDS units are engineered with precision—featuring inlet/outlet sections, bypass arrangements, control and safety valves, spray nozzle systems, and advanced instrumentation—all designed to handle steam conditions up to 110 kg/cm² and 535°C, while minimizing noise and vibration.

Our integrated boiler and control systems are trusted by over 200 industries, including prominent sugar mills and cogen plants in India and across international markets like Sudan, Uganda, Philippines, Kenya, and Ethiopia. Backed by a skilled engineering team.

Tailor-Made Control Solutions for Efficient Boiler Automation

We specialize in delivering tailored automation solutions designed to meet the unique needs of a diverse range of process industries. Our expertise encompasses everything from implementing single-loop digital controllers and compact PLCs for small-scale systems, to deploying sophisticated Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) and Distributed Control Systems (DCS) for complex, multi-loop control environments. Whether your operations require basic automation or fully integrated control architectures, we provide scalable, reliable, and efficient solutions. At the heart of our service offering is our core specialization in Boiler Control Automation. We are committed to ensuring your boiler systems operate with maximum efficiency, safety, and reliability, optimizing fuel usage and maintaining consistent steam quality across a variety of load conditions. Our automation strategies are designed to align with your process goals while meeting the highest standards of operational excellence. To guarantee continuous system performance
